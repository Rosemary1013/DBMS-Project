How to Use Insurix

A simple, 3-step guide to securing your future.

1. Create an Account

Register via the 'Customer Access' portal. Ensure you provide accurate contact and personal details for an easy onboarding process.

2. Choose an Insurance

Explore our tailored policies on the homepage. Click 'Enroll Now' and register a nominee beneficiary to securely initialize your plan.

3. Pay & Claim Online

Pay your policy premiums seamlessly from your dashboard. Once your policy duration concludes, you can submit a one-click maturity claim!
