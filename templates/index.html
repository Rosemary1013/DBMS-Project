Platform Features

Everything You Need
To Manage Insurance

A fully integrated system covering every aspect of insurance management — from policy creation to claim approval.

Policy Management

Browse Health, Life and Vehicle policies. Compare premiums, coverage amounts and durations before taking out a policy.

Claims Processing

File claims online and track their approval status in real time. Admins can approve or reject claims with full audit trail.

Payment Tracking

Pay premiums by Online, Card or Cash. View your full payment history and track all transactions with dates and amounts.

Nominee Protection

Add and manage nominees for each policy with their contact details, ensuring your loved ones are protected at all times.

Agent System

Dedicated agents are assigned to policies. Agents can verify policy details and manage their assigned customer requests.

Admin Control Panel

Full system oversight for admins — manage customers, verify policies, approve claims and monitor all payments in one place.

Who It's For

Three Roles.
One Powerful System.

Customer

Manage Your Policies

Register, browse available insurance plans, take out policies and stay on top of your claims and payments — all in one dashboard.

  • View & take out policies
  • File and track claims

Agent

Handle Policy Requests

Agents are assigned to specific policies and are responsible for verifying customer details and managing policy-related requests.

  • View assigned policies
  • Verify customer details
  • Process policy requests
  • Update agent profile
  • View claim statuses
Admin

Full System Control

Admins have complete visibility and control over the entire system — from managing users and policies to approving claims and payments.

  • Manage all customers
  • Approve / reject claims
  • Verify policy agents
  • Monitor all payments
  • Full audit access
